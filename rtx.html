Frequently Asked Questions

Which courses can I get a scholarship on through ANTHE?

ANTHE gives students a chance to get up to 100%* Scholarship on Medical (NEET), Engineering (JEE) and Foundation Classroom courses

Can class 7th studying students register for ANTHE?

Yes. If you are currently studying in Class 7, you can register for ANTHE. You will be eligible to take admission in courses in the next academic year.

I am a 12th Passed student. Can I register for ANTHE?

Yes. Class 12th Studying and Passed students are eligible to register for ANTHE. 12th Passed students can get admission in Aakash BYJU’S Repeater Classroom Courses in the next academic year.

Are there any practice tests available for ANTHE preparation?

Once registered, students will be able to access mock tests by logging into their profile.
