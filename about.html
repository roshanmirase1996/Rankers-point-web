Director's Message

Dear Students,

Life is an optimum platform of opportunities, conferring a constant possibility of blissful venture. What can be a more adventurous feat than education? Education is the most democratic segment of our system which offers unconditional and impartial possibility to exhibit our potential.

For more than two decades, we have been extending our endeavor towards catering to quality education. As our name signifies, Rankers Point, Yavatmal aims at attaining the pinnacles of success in this pious field of education without forgetting our basic values.

Rankers Point, Yavatmal always believes in the balanced blend of modern and traditional processes. Thus, we encourage innovative concepts and duly reward them through various screening tests. We accept that natural talent is like a ship, and without seasoned captaincy, it can be drowned. With relentless efforts of our professionals, we shape, sculpt, and channelize every student's potential towards productivity in the most rational and scientific manner.

Rankers Point, Yavatmal is obliged to provide quality coaching for various entrance examinations like JEE (Main & Advance), NEET, MHT-CET, with the class infrastructure facility. We also provide TEST SERIES (ONLINE/CLASSROOM/DLP) for NEET/JEE/MHT-CET. Morality is the core of our existence, and to nurture it, we have constant observation towards every student's behavioral pattern.
